Our Story

Where Italian tradition meets Scottish warmth

Welcome to Amarone

Nestled in the charming town of Hawick in the Scottish Borders, Amarone Italian Restaurant brings the authentic flavours of Italy to your table. Named after the celebrated Amarone wine from Veneto, our restaurant is a celebration of Italian culinary heritage.

Every dish we serve tells a story — from our hand-rolled pasta made fresh daily, to our carefully sourced ingredients that honour both Italian tradition and the finest Scottish produce. Our kitchen is led by passionate chefs who believe that great food starts with great ingredients and ends with love.

Whether you're joining us for a relaxed lunch, a special dinner, or a celebration with family and friends, we promise you an experience that is genuinely Italian in every way.

What We Offer

From antipasti to dolci, every course is crafted with care

🍝

Fresh Pasta

Handmade daily using traditional methods. From classic tagliatelle al ragù to delicate ravioli filled with ricotta and spinach — each plate is a labour of love.

🍕

Wood-Fired Pizza

Our dough is slow-fermented for 48 hours, then blistered in our wood-fired oven. The result? Light, crispy, and unmistakably Italian.

🥩

Secondi & Grill

From tender Scottish beef tagliata to pan-seared sea bass with capers and lemon, our main courses showcase the best of land and sea.

🥗

Antipasti & Starters

Begin your meal with our bruschetta, burrata, or a selection of Italian cured meats and cheeses from artisan producers.

🍷

Wine & Cocktails

An extensive Italian wine list featuring everything from crisp Pinot Grigio to bold Barolo, plus classic Italian cocktails — Aperol Spritz, Negroni, and more.

🍰

Dolci & Desserts

End on a sweet note with our homemade tiramisù, panna cotta, or a classic affogato made with rich Italian espresso.
