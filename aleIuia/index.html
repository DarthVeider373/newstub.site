About us

Approachable, organised and built around confectionery.

Based in Huddersfield, Confex Confectionery Limited works with customers who need a straightforward confectionery supplier with clear communication and a practical understanding of sweet ranges, packing and trade requirements.

The business is centred on sugar confectionery and chocolate-related supply, with a service style that suits independent retailers, event organisers, hospitality operators and trade partners looking for dependable support.
